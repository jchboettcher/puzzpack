PuzzPack

Original by Brandon Sterner of Detached Solutions

Clone and extension levels by Jack Boettcher

Every real TI-84 enthusiast remembers PuzzPack. This is an HTML clone of the classic calculator app with all 4 minigames. The game design and levels are taken directly from that, but I've added some levels of my own. If you still have a working TI-84, I highly recommend trying it out there too.

The console below is meant to emulate the experience on a TI-84 as closely as possible-- hence the low resolution and delay in animation. I prefer this mode because it reminds me much more of the original, but for those of you who want a cleaner interface, hit ENTER to toggle between the two views.
